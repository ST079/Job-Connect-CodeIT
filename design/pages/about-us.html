Our Story

Founded in 2018 with a vision to simplify job hunting in Nepal's digital age, Kaam Yeta Hai has grown to become one of the most trusted job portals in the country.

2018 - The Beginning

Founded with a vision to simplify job hunting in Nepal's digital age. Started with a small team in Bhaktapur focused on connecting local talent with opportunities.

2020 - Technology Milestone

Introduced our AI-powered job matching algorithm, revolutionizing how candidates find relevant opportunities and how companies discover talent.

2021 - National Expansion

Expanded operations to 25+ cities across Nepal.

2023 - Present Day

Now serving 500,000+ job seekers and 5,000+ companies with a team of 100+ professionals dedicated to transforming India's job market.
